About Me

I have worked in manufacturing for the last twenty-two years, with the last 11 being in manufacturing and quality engineering. In May of 2021, my wife and I moved from our home in Tennessee to be closer to her family in South Jordan, Utah. After so many years in manufacturing, I believed this move would be a good time for a fresh start in a new career. After searching jobs in the area, I found that there were a high number of coding and web development jobs. I have always had a fascination with computers and thought that if I could break into this industry, it would be just the change I was looking for. I enrolled in a coding bootcamp through the University of Utah in July of 2021. I will be graduating from the program in January of 2022, at which time, I will be looking to join a company to put my new skills to the test.
